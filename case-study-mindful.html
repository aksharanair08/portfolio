Healthcare

Mindful

Mental Health Platform

A compassionate digital experience that connects users with resources, journaling, and professional support while maintaining privacy and reducing stigma.

Overview

Mindful is a mental health and wellness platform that needed a complete product and brand refresh. The client wanted an interface that felt safe, calm, and inclusive—avoiding clinical or cold aesthetics while still feeling credible and professional.

We focused on soft typography, muted colors with sage and cream accents, and clear hierarchy so users could find journaling, mood tracking, and therapist matching without feeling overwhelmed.

The Challenge

  • Balancing warmth with professionalism and trust
  • Designing for vulnerable moments (crisis support, first-time seekers)
  • Making complex flows (matching, scheduling) feel simple
  • Strict privacy and accessibility requirements

Research & Discovery

We ran co-design workshops with mental health professionals and user interviews with people who had used similar apps. Key themes: users wanted control over how much they shared, optional anonymity, and a "soft entry"—small steps like daily check-ins before committing to sessions. We also aligned with WCAG 2.1 AA and considered reduced-motion and low-stimulus preferences.

Solution

We created a home experience centered on a gentle "How are you today?" prompt and progressive disclosure. Onboarding allowed users to set boundaries (what to be reminded about, when). The therapist-matching flow used cards and filters without feeling like a form. We introduced a consistent design system with a sage-and-cream palette and rounded, approachable components.
