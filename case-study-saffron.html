Saffron

Restaurant Booking Experience

Brand identity and end-to-end booking experience for a premium restaurant group, from discovery to reservation and post-dining engagement.

Overview

Saffron is a growing restaurant group known for seasonal menus and warm service. They needed a cohesive brand and a seamless reservation flow that reflected their premium positioning without feeling stuffy. The project spanned brand guidelines, website, and a dedicated booking experience.

We aimed to capture the warmth of dining at Saffron—rich tones, elegant typography, and a booking flow that felt like an invitation rather than a form.

The Challenge

  • Unifying multiple locations under one brand with distinct personality
  • Reducing no-shows and last-minute cancellations through better UX
  • Balancing premium feel with mobile-first, fast booking
  • Integrating with existing POS and reservation backend

Research & Discovery

We shadowed host staff and analyzed reservation data to see where users dropped off. Key findings: guests wanted to see real availability without multiple steps, and they valued dietary filters and special-occasion notes. We also ran preference tests on visual direction—warm earth tones and serif typography resonated most with the target demographic.

Solution

We defined a brand system with a custom palette (saffron, cream, deep green) and Playfair Display for headlines. The booking flow was reduced to: choose location → date & time (with live availability) → party size & notes → confirm. We added optional reminders and a post-meal feedback loop that increased repeat bookings. The site and app shared one design system for consistency.
