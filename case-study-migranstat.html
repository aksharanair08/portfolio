MIGRANSTAT

Empowering individuals battling migraines through intelligent tracking, pattern recognition, and personalized recommendations.

Welcome to the forefront of migraine management with MIGRANSTAT—an innovative app that combines technological innovation with human-centered design to ease the burden of migraine sufferers worldwide. MIGRANSTAT is more than an app; it's a comprehensive toolkit built to understand, analyze, and reduce migraine triggers.

By tracking key factors such as sleep patterns, dietary habits, and environmental conditions like temperature changes, MIGRANSTAT provides valuable insights into migraine episodes. Users can log duration, intensity, and symptoms—creating a rich dataset for meaningful analysis.

Beyond data tracking, MIGRANSTAT leverages advanced algorithms to uncover patterns in users' lifestyles, identifying triggers such as stress, diet, weather shifts, and sleep disturbances. With these insights, the app delivers personalized recommendations and actionable strategies—from stress management techniques to dietary adjustments and improved sleep hygiene—supporting users on their journey toward migraine relief.

Key Features

Intuitive Diary Interface

Users can effortlessly log details about their migraine attacks, including duration, intensity, associated symptoms, and potential triggers.

Comprehensive Tracking

Monitor sleep patterns, dietary intake, and environmental factors such as temperature variations to identify correlations with migraine episodes.

Pattern Recognition

Advanced algorithms analyze user data to identify recurring patterns and potential triggers—stress, diet, weather, sleep disturbances.

Personalized Recommendations

Tailored advice and lifestyle recommendations—stress management, dietary modifications, and sleep hygiene practices.

Progress Tracking

Visualize improvements in migraine frequency and severity, and track the effectiveness of implemented lifestyle changes over time.

User Research

Before diving into the UI/UX design process, extensive user research was conducted to understand the needs, pain points, and preferences of migraine sufferers. Through surveys, interviews, and usability tests, valuable insights were gathered regarding the features and functionalities desired in a migraine tracking app. Key findings highlighted the importance of simplicity, customization options, and real-time data visualization for effective migraine management.

Questionnaire

Design Goals

Based on the user research findings, the following design goals were established for MIGRANSTAT:

Intuitiveness

Ensure the app is easy to navigate and understand, even for users with limited technical expertise.

Customization

Provide users the ability to customize tracking parameters and preferences according to their individual needs.

Real-time Feedback

Enable users to receive immediate feedback on their tracked data and insights into potential migraine triggers.

Personalization

Deliver personalized recommendations and tips tailored to each user's specific migraine patterns and lifestyle factors.

Accessibility

Ensure accessibility features are integrated to accommodate users with disabilities or impairments.

About the App

MIGRANSTAT guides users through calendar management, personal insights, migraine tracking, and daily recommendations. Browse the app screens and flows below.

Calendar & Inquiry Tabs

Upon launching the app, users see the calendar and inquiry tabs. The notes section lets users record breakfast, lunch, and dinner to identify migraine triggers. Local ambient temperatures are tracked to understand the relationship between temperature changes and migraine episodes.

Me Tab — Personal Dashboard

Users get a snapshot of personal data, energy levels, and statistical insights, plus the ability to monitor factors contributing to their most recent migraine episode.

Calendar Tab — Monthly Overview

The calendar tab provides a comprehensive overview of the preceding month, including pertinent information regarding the user's most recent migraine episode.

Stats Tab — Attack Tracking

Shows how many hours have passed since the last migraine attack and offers an option to track a new migraine attack.

Tips Tab — Daily Recommendations

A curated selection of daily recommendations aimed at preventing or mitigating migraine severity, derived from analysis of user data before and after each migraine occurrence.
