LearnSpace

E-Learning Dashboard

A unified dashboard for course creators and learners that simplifies progress tracking, content discovery, and engagement—making learning feel focused and achievable.

Overview

LearnSpace is an ed-tech platform used by universities and corporate training teams. The existing dashboard was cluttered and didn't differentiate clearly between learner and instructor views. Our brief was to redesign the core dashboard so that learners could see their progress at a glance and instructors could manage courses and analytics without context-switching.

We focused on clarity, hierarchy, and a calm visual system that wouldn't compete with course content.

The Challenge

  • One product serving learners and instructors with very different mental models
  • Information overload: courses, deadlines, certificates, discussions in one place
  • Need to support both desktop (primary) and tablet use in classrooms
  • Accessibility and readability for long study sessions

Research & Discovery

We interviewed learners and instructors and ran card-sorting and task-based usability tests. Learners wanted "what's next" and "how am I doing" front and center; instructors wanted quick access to grading, analytics, and course settings. We defined two dashboard modes (Learner / Instructor) with a clear toggle and a shared design language so the product felt like one cohesive experience.

Solution

We introduced a role-aware dashboard: learners see a "Continue learning" hero, progress rings per course, and upcoming deadlines. Instructors see course cards with key metrics and quick actions. We used a sage-and-cream palette with high contrast for text, generous spacing, and collapsible sections so power users could customize their view. A consistent sidebar and header work across both modes for familiarity.
